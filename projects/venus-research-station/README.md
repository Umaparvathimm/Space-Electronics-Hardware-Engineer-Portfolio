## Project Overview
The **Venus Research Station (VRS)** project was a large, interdisciplinary **conceptual mission study** conducted at the **University of WÃ¼rzburg**.

The goal was to design a **long-duration atmospheric research station** operating within the Venusian atmosphere, supported by deployable scout probes, to address key open questions in Venus science, atmospheric dynamics, and planetary habitability.

The study followed **ECSS Phase 0 / early Phase A** methodology, covering mission definition, requirements analysis, subsystem design, trade studies, and system-level integration.  
The final report was **published in the university library** as a formal technical document.
